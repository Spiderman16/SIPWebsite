Scoring Glove

This project is involved in sports for keeping track of player scores.

This is achieved through a glove that you wear and the motion of raising your hand and displaying how many fingers you want to add up which is added to a player's score.

This project was inspired by a situation going on at an IBJJF tournament. The situation was when the ref was having problem with the score keeper not keeping track of points then a thought came to my hand on how can I get where just the ref keeps track of point and that is where I produced the idea of the scoring glove.
